fix: loosen passcode validation to allow special characters

Allow any printable non-whitespace characters in passcodes (e.g. wingman2026!).
Previously restricted to A-Z and 0-9 only, which rejected passwords with ! @ # etc.

Changes:
- update-passcode.ts: remove toUpperCase and alphanumeric regex, allow 6-30 chars with no spaces
- validate-passcode.ts: remove toUpperCase for case-sensitive matching (preserves special chars)
- AccessControlTab.tsx: remove client-side alphanumeric guard, stop auto-uppercasing input, raise maxLength to 30

Users entering the passcode must now match case exactly (wingman2026! != WINGMAN2026!).
